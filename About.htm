About Us

Founded by two former NASA software engineers, Blue Elephant Web Development, LLC. emerged as the product of 20 years of collective experience in systems engineering, software development and project management.. The expertise and experience at Blue Elephant Web Development encompasses everything from mission critical systems for NASA's space shuttle program to comprehensive Point-of-Sale and Restaurant Management Systems.  

What we've learned, in a nutshell, is this: Build exactly what a client needs — no more and no less — and do it on time, on a budget, and with good cheer. Do it right the first time by putting the effort and energy into Step One of the process: determining the scope and purpose of the project.

In 2006, Blue Elephant Web Development teamed up with McLane Advanced Technologies, LLC in order to broaden our scope an extend our professional influence into verticle markets. McLane Advanced Technologies, LLC (MAT) is an Information Technology (IT) and Logistics company providing services to both government and commercial entities. MAT is owned by Drayton McLane, Jr. whose family founded the wholesale grocery distribution business known as McLane Company, Inc. A forerunner in implementing IT into the commercial grocery logistics industry, he has nearly 40 years experience in logistics that turned a family grocery business into a multi-billion dollar global enterprise by creating and automating logistics software and management techniques.
